About this dog

Five hundred placements, four coats. Toy Poodle, atelier-bred.

The Toy Poodle is bred at the atelier from a closed line. Each dog spends roughly nine months in the house — sequencing in Lyon, whelping in Paris, eleven weeks of nursery socialisation — before it is released to its purchaser. The Brown coat is hand-finished and verified against the reference swatch before shipment.

The Toy Poodle is small enough to travel in a structured carrier and quiet enough to attend most private interiors. We do not recommend the dog for households with children under six.

The first year

What the house provides, and what is asked of the household.

  1. Week 0 · Delivery

    White-glove handover

    Climate-controlled courier. Charm Set boxed separately. Signed by purchaser only.

  2. Week 0 · On receipt

    Care Covenant signature

    The Care Covenant and Care Manual are included in the delivery. Completion requires the purchaser's signature, returned to Client Services. Subsequent household care is the household's responsibility under the Covenant.

  3. Month 1 — 12 · Client obligations

    Vaccinations and grooming

    Vaccinations on the standard schedule. Grooming every six weeks to maintain the house curl index. Conducted by the household's own veterinarian and groomer; records submitted to the House for Lineage Certificate annotation.

  4. Ongoing

    Concierge assigned

    An assigned Concierge holds the dog's record. Available through Client Services during published office hours, by switchboard or letter. Re-housing under §6, where applicable, is conducted through the Concierge.
