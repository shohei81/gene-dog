About this dog

A working line. Designed for sustained physical activity. Vocalisation is frequent and is not modifiable.

The Siberian Husky is bred at the atelier from a Mk. II working line. The dog is designed for sustained physical activity; a household without a daily plan for two hours of exercise is unlikely to find this dog acclimatising well.

Vocalisation is frequent and is not modifiable. The Husky line accounts for thirty-one per cent of all returns received by the house in the last twelve months. We strongly encourage the complimentary acclimatisation visit to take place before, not after, reservation is finalised.

The first year

What the house provides, and what is asked of the household.

  1. Week 0 · Delivery

    White-glove handover

    Climate-controlled courier. Charm Set boxed separately. Signed by purchaser only.

  2. Week 1 · Home

    Acclimatisation visit & exercise plan

    A Concierge attends the household to confirm the daily exercise plan. Plans below 120 minutes are flagged for review.

  3. Month 1 — 12

    Vaccinations, recall conditioning, secure-perimeter audit

    Recall conditioning is provided through the first year but is not warranted. A secure-perimeter inspection of the household garden is conducted at month 3.

  4. Ongoing

    Concierge on call

    A named Concierge is assigned for the lifetime of the dog. The Husky line carries a published twelve-month return rate of 31%.
